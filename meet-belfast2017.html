Accommodations and Expenses

While the OSSOS team will not be able to assist with any expenses related to your transport to and from Beaune, we have assembled funding that will cover the following expenses for participants:

  1. No registration fee, so coffee breaks and meeting room fees are covered.

  2. Hotel accomodation in the conference hotel (prepaid by us) for the nights of Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday. The meeting hotel is the Hostellerie de Bretonniere, on the south edge of the main ring of the old city. Hotel web site here, but participants have no need to contact the hotel as all services are negotiated in bulk. Contact ossoscore if you have any questions or needs.

  3. Hotel accomodation will include breakfast the morning after each night's lodging. Lunch will be provided on Thursday and Friday. (Dinners will not be covered).

Travel information

The simplest ways to get to Belfast are:

  1. Fly to Belfast International Airport (BFS) or Belfast City Airport (BHD).

  2. If arriving at BFS, take bus Airport Express 300 to Belfast Europa Buscentre. Buy return ticket (£10.50, valid 1 month) if leaving from same airport. 30-45 min journey time. Details: https://www.belfastairport.com/to-from. Alternatively take a taxi (£30-£40, about 40 min each way).

  3. If arriving at BHD, take bus Airport Express 300 to Belfast Europa Buscentre. Buy return ticket (£3.80, valid 1 month) if leaving from same airport. About 15 min journey time. Details: http://www.belfastcityairport.com/To-From-the-Airport. Alternatively take a taxi (£10-£15, about 15 min each way).

  4. If taking the bus to Belfast city centre, you can then take a taxi from Europa Buscentre to your hotel. About £5 and 5 min ride.

OTHER TRANSPORT OPTIONS: You can hire a car at either airport.
